Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about conversational surveys

How does the AI understand natural language responses?

Our AI uses advanced natural language processing to understand context, handle multiple answers in one message, resolve conflicts, and extract structured data from conversational responses. It's trained to handle edge cases like vague answers, off-topic responses, and questions skipped by respondents.

Is this really better than traditional surveys?

Yes. Conversational forms get 3x higher completion rates because they feel personal and engaging. Without visible multiple choice options, responses are more authentic and unbiased. You also get richer qualitative data that reveals the "why" behind answers.

What types of questions can I ask?

Any type! Text responses, multiple choice, ratings, yes/no questions, and numbers. The AI automatically infers the question type from your description and handles the data extraction accordingly.

How do I analyze the responses?

View responses in your dashboard with charts and insights. Export data as CSV or JSON to use with Excel, Google Sheets, or your analytics tools. All conversational responses are automatically structured into clean, analyzable data.

Is respondent data private?

Completely anonymous by design. We don't collect personal information or require respondents to create accounts. Only you can see your survey responses, and data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

How much does it cost?

Start completely free! Create and test unlimited surveys. We only charge when people respond to your surveys, starting at $0.10 per completed response. No monthly fees, no setup costs - pay only for what you use.
