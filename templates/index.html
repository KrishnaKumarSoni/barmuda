Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about conversational surveys

How does the AI understand natural language responses?

Our AI uses GPT-4o-mini with specialized tools to understand context, handle multiple answers in one message, resolve conflicts, and extract structured data from conversational responses. It's trained to handle edge cases like vague answers, off-topic responses, and questions people skip.

How do I embed surveys on my website?

Copy-paste one line of JavaScript code and get a floating chat widget on your site. No complex setup, no iframes, no CSP issues. The widget works on any website and matches your brand colors.

What types of questions can I ask?

Any type! Text responses, multiple choice, ratings, yes/no questions, and numbers. The AI automatically infers question types from your description. We also provide 12+ templates for common use cases like customer feedback and employee surveys.

How do I analyze the responses?

View responses in your dashboard with charts, word clouds, and insights. Export data as CSV or JSON for Excel, Google Sheets, or analytics tools. All conversational responses are automatically structured into clean, analyzable data.

Is respondent data private?

Yes. Respondents stay anonymous - no accounts or personal info required. We only collect device fingerprints to prevent duplicates. Your survey data is encrypted, stored securely, and only accessible to you.

How much does it cost?

Start free with 100 conversations per month and 3 forms. Paid plans start at $19/month for 1,000 conversations with unlimited forms, embedding, and custom colors. No per-response charges - simple monthly pricing.
