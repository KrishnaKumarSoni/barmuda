How does the AI understand natural language responses?

Our AI uses GPT-4o-mini with specialized tools to understand context, handle multiple answers in one message, resolve conflicts, and extract structured data from conversational responses. It's trained to handle edge cases like vague answers, off-topic responses, and questions people skip.

How do I embed surveys on my website?

Copy-paste one line of JavaScript code and get a floating chat widget on your site. No complex setup, no iframes, no CSP issues. The widget works on any website and matches your brand colors.

How does the AI handle follow-up questions?

The AI asks smart follow-ups based on user responses. If someone says "it's confusing," it asks what specifically is confusing. If they mention price, it digs into their budget and value perception. No generic canned responses - it adapts to get the insights you need.
